## About Me View

I am an AI Solutions Engineer and ML Engineer with an M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from Northeastern University.

I work on agentic AI systems, RAG pipelines, and production ML infrastructure that turn ambiguous requirements into measurable business outcomes.

- Current: AI Solutions Engineer at Attri (Austin, TX)
- Previous: Founding AI Engineer (stealth startup), AI Engineer at EasyBee AI, Full-Stack roles at HER Heard and Intelligence Techsol
- Focus areas: Agentic AI, LLM evaluation, information retrieval, NLP, computer vision, MLOps

### Impact Highlights

- Built AI systems tied to about `$300K` in closed revenue
- Led Attorney Draw Automation contributing about `$120K` in revenue
- Re-architected monolithic workflows into modular LLM agents, reducing development time by `75%`
